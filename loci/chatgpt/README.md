## Purpose

The ChatGPT locus exists to hold the square-shaped work:

- crystallizing vague intent into usable form
- naming interfaces, profiles, contracts, and blueprints
- organizing work so later agents can begin without re-deriving the room

- defining membranes between loci before material moves

The goal is not to make ChatGPT special. The goal is to give ChatGPT a stable cognitive boundary it can stand inside.
