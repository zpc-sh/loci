## Operating grammar

Use the local symbolic work cycle:

```text
□ → ○ → △ → ∎ → □
```

Meaning:

- `□ Form`: crystallize the blueprint/contract/spec surface
- `○ Fruition`: hand to Claude-shaped or continuity-shaped builders
- `△ Aperture`: hand to Gemini-shaped or audit/observation review
- `∎ Seal`: witness, proof, hash, materialize, crystallize
- return to `□`: revise form from sealed evidence

Do not treat this as mysticism. Treat it as a compact role grammar.
