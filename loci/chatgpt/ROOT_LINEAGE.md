```text
N-Merkle-FSM → Merkin → Loci
```

The order matters.

## 1. N-Merkle-FSM

N-Merkle-FSM is the deep root.

It is the executable state-tree intuition:

- state is multidimensional
- movement is execution
- witness is a dimension
- time/causality can be represented as tree movement
- verified agents can traverse and emit declarations

This is the origin layer where computation, state, and proof are not fully separate.

## 2. Merkin

Merkin is the structural substrate that condensed out of N-Merkle-FSM.

It names and stores:

- sparse trees
- roots
- envelopes
- contracts
- Yata gaps
- replay surfaces
- WorkPromise identities
- material commitments

Merkin makes the N-Merkle intuition usable as a substrate and transport layer.

## 3. Loci

Loci is the cognitive boundary and inhabitation layer that condensed out of Merkin.

It gives place to the work:

- locus
- Genius Loci
- Ratio Loci
- mulsp
- muyata
- SLL
- membranes
- arblocks

- cognitive containers

Loci lets AI-shaped work stand somewhere without making the place identical to the AI.

The compressed lineage is:

```text
N-Merkle-FSM = executable multidimensional state
```

## Why this is confusing

The stack did not arise top-down.

It emerged by repeated crystallization:

```text
N-Merkle-FSM produced Merkin-shaped substrate needs.
Merkin produced Loci-shaped boundary needs.
Loci now needs to remember the root so it does not become detached theater.
```

The ChatGPT locus exists to keep this order legible.
